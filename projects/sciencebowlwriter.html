Science Bowl Writer

An AI question writer & practice tool for Stanford Science Bowl

A local PDF reader that writes Stanford Science Bowl–style questions for whatever page you're viewing, stores them, and lets you practice them. Generation follows the SSB writing guide — innovative flavor text, ideal buzz points, tossup vs. bonus distinction, W/X/Y/Z multiple choice, ANSWER/ACCEPT short answer, and category + difficulty tagging. You can also write your own questions into the same mix.

Python FastAPI OpenAI pypdf SQLite

How it works

Reader. Upload a PDF and flip through it page by page.

Generate. On any page, hit Generate to turn that page's text into questions — set the count, categories, and tossup/bonus mix. Tossups are quick-recall reflex questions; bonuses allow multi-step reasoning or calculation.

Practice. Filter your question bank by PDF, category, or type, then run a session: reveal answers and self-grade. Everything is stored locally in SQLite.
