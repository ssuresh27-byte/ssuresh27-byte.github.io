Concept Tutor

Open a book on one side, talk to a tutor on the other

A PDF reader paired with an AI tutor. The tutor reads each page as an image — so it understands diagrams, structures, and equations, not just text — mines the testable concepts on that page, and has a natural conversation about them: it explains, answers your questions, and probes with its own. Your understanding of each concept is tracked as a number that quietly steers the difficulty of what it asks next.

React TypeScript FastAPI OpenAI Vision PyMuPDF SQLite

How it works

See the page, not the text. PyMuPDF renders each PDF page to an image that serves as both the in-app viewer and the “screenshot” sent to a vision model, so figures and math come through intact.

Concepts, modeled simply. Each concept holds one number, understanding ∈ [0,1], starting at 0. When a message actually demonstrates a concept (an answer or piece of reasoning, not just a question), the model scores it and blends it in: understanding ← 0.6·score + 0.4·understanding.

Difficulty that adapts. Questions climb with mastery — recall, then apply, then analyze, then synthesize — and each page opens on the concept you understand least.
